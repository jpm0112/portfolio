About Me

I'm a fifth-year PhD student in Industrial and Systems Engineering at Auburn University, with expertise at the intersection of operations research, machine learning, and mathematical optimization.

From optimizing military aircraft scheduling for the U.S. Air Force to predicting freight rates with explainable AI, I thrive on challenges that require both analytical rigor and creative problem-solving. My work spans collaborations with Frontier Technology Inc., the Department of Homeland Security, and Norway's NTNU.

Originally from Chile, I bring a multicultural perspective and am fluent in both Spanish and English. I lead the INFORMS student chapter and am passionate about bridging academia and industry.
